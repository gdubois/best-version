About Best Version

Welcome to Best Version — a passion project born from frustration and fueled by love for gaming history.

I created this site because I was tired of seeing modern remakes that don't hold a candle to the original versions. Too often, developers try to "improve" what wasn't broken, losing the magic that made these games special in the first place.

"How many hours have I wasted trying to find consensus on whether Symphony of the Night is best on PSX, PSP, or Saturn? This site exists so you don't have to."

Best Version isn't about ranking games — it's about finding THE BEST version of each game. We dig deep into patches, mods, and platform differences to ensure you know exactly how to experience these classics at their absolute peak.

This is a solo project driven by one mission: help players discover the definitive way to play gaming's greatest titles, complete with the best patches, mods, and technical recommendations available today.
